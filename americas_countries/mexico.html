Mexico 🇲🇽

← Back to Americas

Mexico is a vibrant country known for its rich culture, delicious cuisine, and stunning landscapes. From the ancient ruins of Chichen Itza to the bustling streets of Mexico City, it offers a unique blend of history and modernity.

Top Attractions

  • Chichen Itza
  • Mexico City Historic Center
  • Cancun Beaches
  • Teotihuacan Pyramids
  • Frida Kahlo Museum

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Mexico City
  • Population: ~129 million
  • Birthplace of chocolate
  • Home to the largest pyramid by volume (Great Pyramid of Cholula)
  • Known for mariachi music and vibrant festivals
