Argentina 🇦🇷

← Back to Americas

Argentina is a land of passion and diversity. From the tango-filled streets of Buenos Aires to the breathtaking landscapes of Patagonia, it offers a unique blend of culture, nature, and history.

Top Attractions

  • Buenos Aires
  • Patagonia
  • Iguazu Falls
  • Mendoza Wine Region
  • Perito Moreno Glacier

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Buenos Aires
  • Population: ~46 million
  • Birthplace of the tango
  • Home to the Andes mountains
  • Known for its beef and Malbec wine
