Ecuador 🇪🇨

← Back to Americas

Ecuador is a land of incredible biodiversity and rich culture. From the Galápagos Islands to the Andean highlands, it offers a unique blend of nature and history.

Top Attractions

  • Galápagos Islands
  • Quito's Historic Center
  • Amazon Rainforest
  • Cotopaxi Volcano
  • Otavalo Market

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Quito
  • Population: ~18 million
  • Named after the equator line that runs through it
  • Home to the world's largest rose production
  • Known for its vibrant indigenous culture and festivals
