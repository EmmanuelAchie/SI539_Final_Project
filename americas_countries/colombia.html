Colombia 🇨🇴

← Back to Americas

Colombia is a vibrant country known for its rich culture, stunning landscapes, and warm people. From the bustling streets of Bogotá to the serene beaches of Cartagena, Colombia offers a diverse range of experiences.

Top Attractions

  • Cartagena's Old Town
  • Medellín's Comuna 13
  • The Coffee Triangle
  • Caño Cristales (River of Five Colors)
  • San Andrés Island

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Bogotá
  • Population: ~52 million
  • Birthplace of Shakira and Gabriel García Márquez
  • Home to the world's largest emerald mines
  • Known for its vibrant festivals like Carnaval de Barranquilla
