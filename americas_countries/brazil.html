Brazil 🇧🇷

← Back to Americas

Brazil is a vibrant country known for its lively culture, stunning landscapes, and iconic landmarks. From the Amazon rainforest to the beaches of Rio de Janeiro, it offers a diverse range of experiences.

Top Attractions

  • Christ the Redeemer
  • Amazon Rainforest
  • Copacabana Beach
  • Salvador's Historic Center
  • Pantanal Wetlands

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Brasília
  • Population: ~216 million
  • Home to the largest rainforest in the world
  • Famous for its annual Carnival festival
  • Known for samba music and dance
