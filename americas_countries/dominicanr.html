Dominican Republic 🇩🇴

← Back to Americas

The Dominican Republic is a Caribbean paradise known for its stunning beaches, vibrant culture, and rich history. From the colonial charm of Santo Domingo to the natural beauty of Punta Cana, it offers a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure.

Top Attractions

  • Punta Cana Beaches
  • Santo Domingo's Colonial Zone
  • Los Haitises National Park
  • Saona Island
  • Jarabacoa Mountains

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Santo Domingo
  • Population: ~11 million
  • Birthplace of merengue music
  • Shares the island of Hispaniola with Haiti
  • Known for its vibrant festivals and warm hospitality
