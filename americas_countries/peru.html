Peru 🇵🇪

← Back to Americas

Peru is a land of ancient wonders and vibrant culture. From the majestic Machu Picchu to the bustling city of Lima, it offers a blend of history, nature, and modern life.

Top Attractions

  • Machu Picchu
  • Lima's Historic Center
  • Lake Titicaca
  • Colca Canyon
  • Nazca Lines

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Lima
  • Population: ~34 million
  • Home to the Nazca Lines
  • Birthplace of the potato
  • Known for its vibrant festivals like Inti Raymi
