Canada 🇨🇦

← Back to Americas

Canada is a vast and diverse country known for its stunning natural landscapes, multicultural cities, and friendly people. From the majestic Rocky Mountains to the vibrant city of Toronto, Canada offers a unique blend of outdoor adventure and urban culture.

Top Attractions

  • Niagara Falls
  • Banff National Park
  • CN Tower in Toronto
  • Old Quebec City
  • Vancouver's Stanley Park
  • Montreal's Old Port

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Ottawa
  • Population: ~40 million
  • Second largest country in the world by land area
  • Home to the longest coastline in the world
  • Official languages: English and French
  • Known for maple syrup and hockey
