South Africa 🇿🇦

← Back to Africa

South Africa is a vibrant country known for its diverse culture, stunning landscapes, and rich history. From the bustling cities to the breathtaking safaris, it offers a unique blend of experiences.

Top Attractions

  • Table Mountain
  • Kruger National Park
  • Robben Island
  • Garden Route
  • Cape Winelands

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Pretoria (administrative), Cape Town (legislative), Bloemfontein (judicial)
  • Population: ~60 million
  • Known as the "Rainbow Nation" for its cultural diversity
  • Home to the world's largest diamond mine
  • Has 11 official languages
