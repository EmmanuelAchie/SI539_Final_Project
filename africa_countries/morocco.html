Morocco 🇲🇦

← Back to Africa

Morocco is a vibrant country known for its rich culture, stunning landscapes, and historic cities. From the bustling markets of Marrakech to the serene beaches of Essaouira, Morocco offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity.

Top Attractions

  • Medina of Marrakech
  • Chefchaouen (Blue City)
  • Sahara Desert
  • Fes - Ancient medina
  • Atlas Mountains

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Rabat
  • Population: ~37 million
  • Home to the oldest university in the world (University of Al Quaraouiyine)
  • Famous for its vibrant textiles and crafts
  • Moroccan cuisine is renowned for dishes like Tagine and Couscous
