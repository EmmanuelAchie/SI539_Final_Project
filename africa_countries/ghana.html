Ghana 🇬🇭

← Back to Africa

Ghana is a vibrant country known for its rich history, diverse culture, and warm hospitality. From the bustling markets of Accra to the historic Cape Coast Castle, Ghana offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity.

Top Attractions

  • Accra - The capital city with vibrant culture and nightlife
  • Cape Coast Castle - A historic site with deep significance
  • Kakum National Park - Famous for its canopy walkway
  • Elmina Castle - Another important historical site

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Accra
  • Population: ~34 million
  • Ghana was the first African country to gain independence from colonial rule in 1957.
  • The country is known for its colorful kente cloth, which is a symbol of African heritage.
  • Ghana is home to the largest artificial lake in the world, Lake Volta.
