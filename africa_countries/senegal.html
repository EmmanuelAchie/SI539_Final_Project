Senegal 🇸🇳

← Back to Africa

Senegal is a vibrant country known for its rich culture, music, and history. From the bustling streets of Dakar to the serene beaches of Gorée Island, it offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity.

Top Attractions

  • Dakar
  • Gorée Island
  • Niokolo-Koba National Park
  • Lake Retba (Pink Lake)
  • Saint-Louis - Colonial charm

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Dakar
  • Population: ~18 million
  • Home to the famous Dakar Rally
  • Known for its vibrant music scene, especially Mbalax
  • Senegalese cuisine is famous for dishes like Thieboudienne
