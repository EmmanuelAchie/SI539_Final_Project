Congo 🇨🇩

← Back to Africa

Congo, officially the Democratic Republic of the Congo, is a country in Central Africa known for its rich biodiversity, vibrant culture, and complex history. It is home to the Congo River, one of the longest rivers in the world, and vast rainforests that are crucial for global ecology.

Top Attractions

  • Virunga National Park
  • Kinshasa
  • Lake Kivu
  • Garamba National Park
  • Salonga National Park

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Kinshasa
  • Population: ~105 million
  • Second largest rainforest in the world
  • Home to endangered mountain gorillas
  • Rich in natural resources, including minerals and timber
