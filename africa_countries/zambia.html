Zambia 🇿🇲

← Back to Africa

Zambia is known for its stunning landscapes, vibrant culture, and incredible wildlife. From the majestic Victoria Falls to the diverse national parks, it offers unforgettable experiences.

Top Attractions

  • Victoria Falls
  • South Luangwa National Park
  • Kafue National Park
  • Lower Zambezi National Park
  • Livingstone - Gateway to Victoria Falls

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Lusaka
  • Population: ~20 million
  • Home to one of the largest waterfalls in the world
  • Over 70 ethnic groups, each with their own unique traditions
  • Known for its rich biodiversity, including the Big Five
