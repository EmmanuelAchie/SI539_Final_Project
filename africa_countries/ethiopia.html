Ethiopia 🇪🇹

← Back to Africa

Ethiopia is a land of ancient history, diverse cultures, and stunning landscapes. From the rock-hewn churches of Lalibela to the Simien Mountains, it offers a unique blend of tradition and natural beauty.

Top Attractions

  • Lalibela Churches
  • Simien Mountains
  • Axum Obelisks
  • Blue Nile Falls
  • Harar Jugol (Old City)

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Addis Ababa
  • Population: ~126 million
  • One of the oldest countries in the world
  • Birthplace of coffee
  • Unique calendar with 13 months
