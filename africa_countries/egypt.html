Egypt 🇪🇬

← Back to Africa

Egypt is a land of ancient wonders, vibrant culture, and timeless beauty. From the iconic pyramids to the bustling streets of Cairo, it offers a unique blend of history and modernity.

Top Attractions

  • Pyramids of Giza
  • Luxor Temple
  • Red Sea Beaches
  • Valley of the Kings
  • Abu Simbel Temples

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Cairo
  • Population: ~110 million
  • Home to one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World
  • The Nile River is the longest river in the world
  • Egyptian cuisine is famous for dishes like Koshari and Ful Medames
