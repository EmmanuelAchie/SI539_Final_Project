Kenya 🇰🇪

← Back to Africa

Kenya is renowned for its stunning landscapes, rich wildlife, and vibrant culture. From the savannahs of the Maasai Mara to the bustling streets of Nairobi, it offers a unique blend of nature and urban life.

Top Attractions

  • Maasai Mara National Reserve
  • Mount Kenya
  • Diani Beach
  • Lake Nakuru National Park
  • Amboseli National Park

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Nairobi
  • Population: ~55 million
  • Home to the Great Rift Valley
  • Birthplace of many world-class long-distance runners
