Nigeria 🇳🇬

← Back to Africa

Nigeria is a vibrant country known for its rich culture, music, and diverse landscapes. From bustling cities to beautiful beaches, it offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity.

Top Attractions

  • Lagos
  • Abuja
  • Yankari National Park
  • Olumo Rock
  • Obudu Mountain Resort

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Abuja
  • Population: ~230 million
  • Most populous country in Africa
  • Known for Nollywood, the second largest film industry in the world
  • Home to diverse cultures and over 500 languages
