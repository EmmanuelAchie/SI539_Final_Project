Czechia 🇨🇿

← Back to Europe

Czechia is known for its fairy-tale architecture and historic charm.

Top Attractions

  • Prague Castle
  • Charles Bridge
  • Old Town Square
  • Cesky Krumlov

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Prague
  • Population: ~10.7 million
  • Famous for beer culture
  • Prague is called "City of a Hundred Spires"
  • Home to the world's oldest operating astronomical clock
