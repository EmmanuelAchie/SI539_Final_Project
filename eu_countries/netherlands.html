Netherlands 🇳🇱

← Back to Europe

The Netherlands is famous for canals, windmills, and cycling culture.

Top Attractions

  • Amsterdam Canals
  • Keukenhof Gardens
  • Anne Frank House
  • Rijksmuseum
  • Van Gogh Museum

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Amsterdam
  • Population: ~17 million
  • Flat landscape below sea level
  • More bikes than people
  • Famous for tulips and cheese
