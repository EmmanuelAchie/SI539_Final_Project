Greece 🇬🇷

← Back to Europe

Greece is the birthplace of democracy and known for its islands, ancient ruins, and Mediterranean beauty.

Top Attractions

  • Acropolis of Athens
  • Santorini
  • Mykonos
  • Delphi

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Athens
  • Population: ~10 million
  • Over 6,000 islands
  • One of the oldest civilizations
  • Famous for Greek mythology
