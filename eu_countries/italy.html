Top Attractions

  • Venice Canals
  • Leaning Tower of Pisa
  • Florence Cathedral
  • Amalfi Coast
  • Vatican City

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Rome
  • Population: ~60 million
  • Home to the Roman Empire
  • Invented pizza and pasta
  • Birthplace of Renaissance art
