Monaco 🇲🇨

← Back to Europe

Monaco is a small luxury city-state known for wealth, casinos, and yachts.

Top Attractions

  • Monte Carlo Casino
  • Prince’s Palace
  • Harbor
  • Oceanographic Museum

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Monaco
  • Population: ~39,000
  • Second smallest country in the world
  • No income tax for residents
  • Famous for the Monaco Grand Prix
