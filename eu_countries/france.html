France 🇫🇷

← Back to Europe

France is known for its rich culture, world-class cuisine, and iconic landmarks. From Paris to the French Riviera, it blends history, art, and modern lifestyle.

Top Attractions

  • Eiffel Tower
  • Louvre Museum
  • French Riviera
  • Mont Saint-Michel
  • Palace of Versailles

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Paris
  • Population: ~68 million
  • Most visited country in the world
  • Famous for over 1,000 types of cheese
  • Birthplace of cinema and fashion
