New Zealand 🇳🇿

← Back to Oceania

New Zealand is renowned for its stunning landscapes, outdoor adventures, and vibrant Maori culture. From the fjords of Milford Sound to the geothermal wonders of Rotorua, it offers a unique blend of natural beauty and cultural richness.

Top Attractions

  • Milford Sound
  • Rotorua Geothermal Area
  • Queenstown
  • Fiordland National Park
  • Waitomo Glowworm Caves
  • Abel Tasman National Park
  • Mount Cook National Park
  • Tongariro Alpine Crossing

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Wellington
  • Population: ~5 million
  • Official languages: English, Maori, NZ Sign Language
  • First country to grant women the right to vote in 1893
  • Home to the Hobbit films
