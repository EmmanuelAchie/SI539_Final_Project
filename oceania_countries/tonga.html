Tonga 🇹🇴

← Back to Oceania

Tonga is a Polynesian kingdom known for its stunning beaches, vibrant culture, and friendly people. It offers a unique blend of natural beauty and rich traditions.

Top Attractions

  • Haʻamonga ʻa Maui
  • Blowholes of Tofua
  • Vavaʻu Islands

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Nukuʻalofa
  • Population: ~105,000
  • Only monarchy in the Pacific
  • Home to humpback whales during migration season
