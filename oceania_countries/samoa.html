Samoa 🇼🇸

← Back to Oceania

Samoa is a tropical paradise in the South Pacific, known for its stunning beaches, vibrant culture, and warm hospitality. It offers a unique blend of natural beauty and rich traditions.

Top Attractions

  • To Sua Ocean Trench
  • Lalomanu Beach
  • Alofaaga Blowholes
  • Robert Louis Stevenson Museum
  • Falealupo Rainforest

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Apia
  • Population: ~225,000
  • Independent since 1962
  • Known for traditional tattooing (tatau)
