Fiji 🇫🇯

← Back to Oceania

Fiji is a tropical paradise known for its stunning beaches, vibrant culture, and warm hospitality. It offers a blend of relaxation and adventure in the heart of the South Pacific.

Top Attractions

  • Coral Coast
  • Garden of the Sleeping Giant
  • Fiji Museum
  • Yasawa Islands
  • Navala Village

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Suva
  • Population: ~900,000
  • Over 300 islands make up Fiji
  • Known for its traditional Fijian ceremonies
