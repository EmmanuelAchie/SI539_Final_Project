Australia 🇦🇺

← Back to Oceania

Australia is known for its stunning landscapes, unique wildlife, and vibrant cities. From the Great Barrier Reef to Sydney's iconic Opera House, it offers a blend of natural beauty and modern culture.

Top Attractions

  • Great Barrier Reef
  • Sydney Opera House
  • Uluru (Ayers Rock)
  • Bondi Beach
  • Blue Mountains

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Canberra
  • Population: ~26 million
  • Home to the world's largest coral reef system
  • Has more kangaroos than people
