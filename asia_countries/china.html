China 🇨🇳

← Back to Asia

China is a vast country with a rich history, diverse culture, and stunning landscapes. From the Great Wall to the bustling cities, it offers a unique blend of ancient traditions and modern innovation.

Top Attractions

  • Great Wall of China
  • Forbidden City
  • Terracotta Army
  • Giant Panda Breeding Research Base
  • Li River and Guilin

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Beijing
  • Population: ~1.4 billion
  • Most populous country in the world
  • Home to the world's longest wall
  • Chinese cuisine is famous worldwide, with dishes like Peking duck and dim sum
