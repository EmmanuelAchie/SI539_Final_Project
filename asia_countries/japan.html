Japan 🇯🇵

← Back to Asia

Japan is a fascinating blend of ancient traditions and cutting-edge technology. From the bustling streets of Tokyo to the serene temples of Kyoto, it offers a unique cultural experience.

Top Attractions

  • Tokyo
  • Kyoto
  • Mount Fuji
  • Hiroshima Peace Memorial
  • Osaka Castle

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Tokyo
  • Population: ~126 million
  • Known for its cherry blossom festivals in spring
  • Home to over 200 volcanoes, including Mount Fuji
