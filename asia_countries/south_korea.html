South Korea 🇰🇷

← Back to Asia

South Korea is a dynamic blend of cutting-edge technology and rich cultural heritage. From the bustling streets of Seoul to the serene temples, it offers a unique experience for travelers.

Top Attractions

  • Seoul
  • Busan
  • Jeju Island
  • Gyeongju
  • Changdeokgung Palace

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Seoul
  • Population: ~51 million
  • Known for its vibrant K-pop culture
  • Home to the world's fastest internet speeds
  • Rich in history with UNESCO World Heritage sites like Gyeongju and Changdeokgung Palace
