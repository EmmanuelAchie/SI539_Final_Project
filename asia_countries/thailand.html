Thailand 🇹🇭

← Back to Asia

Thailand is famous for its stunning beaches, vibrant culture, and delicious cuisine. From bustling Bangkok to serene islands, it offers a unique blend of tradition and modernity.

Top Attractions

  • Phuket
  • Bangkok
  • Chiang Mai
  • Ayutthaya
  • Phi Phi Islands

Interesting Facts

  • Capital: Bangkok
  • Population: ~71 million
  • Known as the "Land of Smiles"
  • Home to over 40,000 Buddhist temples
  • Thai cuisine is renowned worldwide, with dishes like Pad Thai and Tom Yum Goong
